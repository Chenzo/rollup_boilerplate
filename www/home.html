What is the Sea Of Thieves Log App

The Sea of Thieves Ship Log is a Web App that you can use on your phone or in your browser to manually track the number of ships you sunk, or record when you've defeated a Kraken or Megalodon. Mark the times you've completed a Skeleton Fleet or a Fort of the Damned
All with a simple click of a button.
